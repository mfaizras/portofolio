# InstrumentasiBK

### Background
InstrumentasiBK was developed to modernize the workflow for guidance and counseling professionals. The client required a digital transition from paper-based or manual non-cognitive assessments to a centralized system. As a freelance developer, my focus was on stabilizing the existing infrastructure and expanding the system's capability to handle complex psychological assessment instruments used in educational environments.

### Overview
The system serves as a comprehensive assessment platform specifically designed for school counselors. It enables the digital distribution of non-cognitive instruments to students, automates the scoring process, and generates structured reports. The primary goal was to ensure that the transition from raw student data to actionable counseling insights was both consistent and mathematically accurate.
